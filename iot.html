Welcome to the Department of Internet of Things

About the Department

The Internet of Things (IoT) Department is at the forefront of innovation, connecting the digital and physical worlds. With a focus on smart systems, real-time data analytics, and IoT security, the department equips students to design and implement intelligent solutions for industries, homes, and cities. We aim to drive the future of connected technology.
