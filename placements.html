PLACEMENTS

2021-25 Batch- Total 25 Students are selected for Tech Mahindra Company


St. Ann’s have signed MoU’s with various MNC’s like Infosys, IBM, Wipro etc. St. Ann’s are conducting various Academic Initiative Programmes with Infosys Campus Connect, Wipro mission 10X, IBM Center for Excellence and The Great Mind Challenge. Regular interaction with other institutions & industries is done to update the database on employment opportunities in national multinational companies like Infosys, IBM, Wipro, TCS, Cognizant, Mahindra-Satyam, ICICI, Reliance, Oracle etc. The Cell has built up good industry – institute interface.

Full-fledged Placement activities have been done at SACET, with a centralized Placement cell exclusively interacting with industry, headed by a full-time Placement Officer with a view to enable the students to get the employment opportunities right in the campus while they pursue their studies. For this, every year reputed Training agencies were identified and training imparted throughout the academic year. With the help of these agencies, Workshops and Communication development programmes were conducted every year.


2021-25 Batch- 2 students selected for Kalvium with 5.5 Lach per annaum

Further to enhance the confidence of the students, Placement cell also has taken the initiative to periodically train students who were weak in communication. This department interacts with reputed organizations all over the country for arranging campus interviews for the placement of final year students. It makes efforts to send most of the students for in-plant training to enhance their technical ability and gain exposure to the practical world.

2021-25 Batch- 3 students selected for Snovaysis with 3.5 Lach per annaum

Industrial visits to reputed companies are arranged to know more details about industrial activities. It also enhances the soft-skill capability of the students through regular training programs. The department counsels the final year students for their higher studies and for getting projects from industries.
